About Us
ETutor is a voluntary tutor system that allows any students to sign up as tutors for other students who come from disadvantaged backgrounds. Our goal is to support as many economically disadvantaged students as possible so that they are given the same opportunities as everyone else. Any students are welcome to sign up as a tutor as there are no requirements to become a tutor. Any volunteers are greatly appreciated and we value the time and effort of each and every tutor.
Hello, we are ETutor
We are here to help disadvantaged students to achieve their academic goals. *


We have tutors available for a wide variety of subjects.
*Students are expected to exercise independent thought and expression. The tutors have the right to ask for the source of coursework content and may or may not be able to assist with it.
